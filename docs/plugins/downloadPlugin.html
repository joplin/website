Download Instructions for
  1. Open Joplin
  2. Navigate to settings (“Tools > Options” on Windows/Linux, “Joplin > Settings” on a Mac)
  3. Click on “plugins” in the sidebar.
  4. Search for “...”.
  5. Click install.

If you know what you're doing, you can alternatively download this plugin's .jpl file . This method of installation, however, will not auto-update the plugin.
